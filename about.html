Our Heritage

About The Aurelian

25 Years of Excellence

A Legacy Built on Extraordinary Experiences

Founded in 2001 by the Aurelia family, The Aurelian began as a vision to create a place where guests didn't just stay — they arrived. Built on a pristine stretch of coastline, our founder Elena Aurelia believed that true luxury lay not in opulence alone, but in the quiet confidence of world-class service.

Over the past 25 years, we have grown from a 40-room boutique property into a full luxury resort — yet our founding commitment has never wavered: every guest leaves feeling more valued than when they arrived.
